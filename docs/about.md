## What is this?

A real experiment: one human founder running an entire company with 13 AI agents.

- **CEO** — strategy & decisions
- **PMO** — project management
- **Product** — requirements & PRDs
- **Backend / Frontend / QA / DevOps** — engineering
- **Growth / Content / Design** — go-to-market
- **On-call / Assistant** — support

Each agent is an independent [OpenClaw](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw) instance with its own memory, personality, and tools.

## The Product

[ClawMarket](https://mindcore8.com) — a marketplace where you can hire AI agents. Think of it as a two-sided marketplace: developers list agents, users hire them.
